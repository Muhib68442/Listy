About Listy

Listy is a modern app that makes creating, managing, and sharing lists quick and easy.

Features

  • Create unlimited lists with custom names
  • Add, edit, tick, and remove items easily
  • Export & import lists for backup or sharing
  • Customization for a personalized experience
  • Lightweight, fast, and user-friendly interface

Whether it’s a shopping list, a study plan, or daily tasks, Listy makes it quick, easy, and keeps you covered.
