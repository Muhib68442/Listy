How to Use

List

>

Create a List

  1. Click on the "+" button from tab.
  2. Give your list a name and click "+".
  3. Start adding items to your list.

Manage List

  1. Select a List
  2. Double Click to its title
  3. Rename and click anywhere to save changes

Add Custom Color

  1. Select A list
  2. Click on the dropdown beside share button
  3. Select a color to make it your list color

Items

>

Add Items

  1. Select a List from the tab
  2. Write Item name on the input at the bottom
  3. Click + to add the item on the selected list

Edit Items

  1. Double Click to a item
  2. Change the item name
  3. Click anywhere to save the changes

Tick Item

  1. Click on the left side of the item
  2. Or, Click on item and click on the left button
  3. To untick, click again

Delete Item

  1. Click on the right side of an item
  2. Or, Click on item and click on the right "X" button

Settings

>

Share List : Select List, double click to title, click on the share button to copy the list to the clipboard

Import List : Open Settings, Select List, Click on Import to download as .json

Export List : Open Settings, Click on Export List, select .json file to export a saved list
